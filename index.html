Biography
Elara Montgomery is a professional model with over 8 years of experience in editorial, commercial, and runway modeling. Known for her warm presence and ability to adapt to diverse styles, she has worked with top photographers and brands worldwide. Elara is passionate about sustainable fashion and often collaborates on projects that promote eco-consciousness in the industry.
